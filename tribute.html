PAWAN KALYAN

Man of Dedication and Commitment

The Story of Pawan Kalyan to Duputy Cheif Minister

    Early Life and Background (1971-1995)

  • 1971: Pawan Kalyan was born on September 2, 1971, in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh, India.
  • 1996: Made his acting debut with the film "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi."

    • Initial Success in Telugu Cinema(1996-2000)

  • 1997: Starred in "Gokulamlo Seetha" and "Suswagatham."
  • 1998: Featured in "Tholi Prema," which won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu.
  • 1999: Acted in "Thammudu," a major commercial success.
  • 2000: Played the lead role in "Badri," another hit film.

    • Establishment as a Leading Actor (2001-2005)

  • 2001: Starred in "Kushi," a blockbuster film.
  • 2003: Acted in "Johnny," which he also directed.
  • 2004: Featured in "Gudumba Shankar," contributing to the screenplay.
  • 2005: Acted in "Balu ABCDEFG," which received positive reviews.

    • Continued Success and Experimentation (2006-2010)

  • 2006: Starred in "Bangaram."
  • 2008: Acted in "Jalsa," directed by Trivikram Srinivas, which became a big hit.
  • 2010: Starred in "Puli," which had high expectations but received mixed reviews.

    • Political Entry and Major Film Successes (2011-2015)

  • 2011: Starred in "Teen Maar."
  • 2012: Acted in "Gabbar Singh," a major commercial success and a turning point in his career.
  • 2013: Starred in "Cameraman Gangatho Rambabu."
  • 2014: Founded the Jana Sena Party on March 14.
  • 2014:Acted in "Attarintiki Daredi," which became one of the highest-grossing Telugu films at that time.
  • 2015: Starred in "Gopala Gopala," a remake of the Hindi film "Oh My God."

    • Active Political Involvement and Film Roles (2016-2020)

  • 2016: Acted in "Sardaar Gabbar Singh."
  • 2017: Starred in "Katamarayudu."
  • 2018: Acted in "Agnyaathavaasi," which had mixed reviews.
  • 2019: Actively campaigned in the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly elections.
    Jana Sena Party won one seat in the elections.
  • 2020: Focused more on political activities and social initiatives.

    • Recent Activities and Contributions(2021-2024)

  • 2021: Made a comeback to films with "Vakeel Saab," a remake of the Hindi film "Pink," which received positive reviews.
  • 2022: Acted in "Bheemla Nayak," another successful film.
  • 2023: Continued his political and film activities, with ongoing involvement in social causes and public service.
  • 2024: Contested in AP Assembly Elections along with TDP and BJP as a Allience
  • 2024: Won 21/21 Assembly Seats and 2/2 Parliament Seats with 100% Strike Rate
  • 2024: Appointed as the Deputy Cheif Minister of Andhra Pradesh...........

    • Philanthropy and Social Contributions

  • Pawan Kalyan has been known for his philanthropic activities, including donations to support disaster relief efforts and assistance to the underprivileged.
  • He has been vocal about social issues and often uses his platform to address problems faced by the common people.
  • His love for nature and environmental concerns is evident through his advocacy for sustainable practices and participation in green initiatives.

    • Summary

    Pawan Kalyan's journey has been marked by his dual careers in cinema and politics, his dedication to social causes, and his efforts to bring about positive change. His film career boasts numerous hits, while his political journey reflects his commitment to public service and social welfare
